Purpose

The Purpose of ""Rattler Market"" Is to promote Florida Agricultural And Mechanical University Buisnesses owned by Students. Our product is a web-based application entitled “Rattler Market'' that will advertise student-owned businesses to a wide audience in hopes to increase product sales or services. The most important aspect of the design is that our product will be a hub or source that markets various businesses within the university.

  • Promote/ Advertise to Widen Audiences
  • Increase Sales/ Serivces
  • Network

Marketing

“Rattler Market” will promote student businesses. Consumers will be able to access details of student owned businesses within Application. Here students would be able to directly connect with student owned businesses.
